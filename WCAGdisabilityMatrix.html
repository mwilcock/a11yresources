The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) are in place to ensure that websites and web applications are built in an accessible manner. The different points within the guidelines help different disabilities and not all points directly impact a user with a specific category of disability or impairment.
Within the Matrix below we have categorised disabilities into four categories. these four categories are Cognitive, Visual, Auditory and Mobility; these disabilities can also be Permanent, Temporary or situational.
